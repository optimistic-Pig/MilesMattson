In The Narrator, you play as a journalist on Mars who interviews Martians and publishes their findings back to Earth.

Controls: WASD to move, left click to interact (either with the Martian to interview, or the desk in the back of the office to write your article).

⚠️ Spoilers — Secret Ending Guide (click to reveal)

To find the secret ending:

  1. Talk to Kortnara (the right of the first two options).
  2. Then talk to Gorp and follow this path:
    • Ask him who he is / ask what he studies
    • Ask about the land of Red Rock and how it's changing
    • Ask about the human base
    • Ask about them just taking the land
    • Ask how they were able to just claim it
    • Either final option
  3. Write the article for Day 2.
  4. Click on the panel behind the desk — you will find and talk to Andrew, activating the true ending.
